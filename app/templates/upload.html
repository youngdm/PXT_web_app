What are PXT Tags?

Peatland eXchange Tags (PXT) are standardized metadata tags that help make peatland research data more discoverable, accessible, and interoperable. This tool helps you apply these tags to your existing datasets.

The PXT system organizes metadata into three levels:

  • Site Level: Location and site characteristics
  • Event Level: Observation and measurement details
  • Dataset Level: Overall collection information
