üå± What is the PXT Web App?

The Peatland eXchange Tags (PXT) Web App is a data processing and standardization platform specifically designed for peatland research and monitoring data. Inspired by HXL Proxy, it serves as a bridge between researchers and standardized data formats, helping to make peatland datasets more discoverable, accessible, and interoperable across research institutions.

Our Mission: Enable seamless data exchange across the peatland research community through standardized metadata tagging, supporting FAIR data principles while respecting researcher data sovereignty.

üöÄ Getting Started

Ready to standardize your peatland data? Here's how to begin:

  1. Prepare your data: Ensure your CSV file has column headers
  2. Upload your file: Use our simple upload interface

  4. Apply PXT tags: Map your columns to standardized tags (coming in Week 2)

  6. Integrate: Use the tagged data in your research workflows
